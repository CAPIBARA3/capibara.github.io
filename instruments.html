Instruments Overview

To explore the mysteries of cosmic rays and their interactions in Earth's orbit, our satellite will be equipped with a suite of advanced instruments designed to detected and analyze high-energy radiation.

Each instrument plays a key role in capturing data, contributing to the overall mission goals.

Charged Particle Detector

This instrument is designed to detect primary cosmic rays by measuring the charge, energy, and trajectory of high-energy particles that enter Earth's atmosphere.

X-ray

This detector captures secondary emissions in the form of X-rays and gamma rays, providing insights into high-energy astrophysical phenomena such as supernovae and black holes.
