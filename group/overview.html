Joan Alcaide-Núñez

×

Joan is the Group Leader of CAPIBARA, focusing on cosmic ray detection instrumentation and outreach. His work includes designing detector systems to enhance data accuracy and mentoring junior team members.

I serve as the Group leader, meaning that I am responsible for the project's planning and scheduling, as well as the relation with PLDSpace's spark program coordinators. My most important task is that of providing information and support to all team members.

Personally, I focus on the scientific application of the data. I've conducted previous research on the Hubble constant using Cepheid and Type Ia SNe. Furthermore, I want to use our mission to investigate the standarization of GRBs. This energetic explosions can be detected very far away, making them a powerful distance indicator with great potential for cosmology.
