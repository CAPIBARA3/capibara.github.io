Our Instruments

Our satellite will be equipped with a suite of advanced instruments designed to detect and analyze high-energy radiation.

Primary Cosmic Ray Detector

This experiment detects cosmic ray protons and alpha-particles. Using a layered structure of detectors, it captures time-of-flight, charge, and energy of cosmic rays for full characterization.

X-ray Observatory

Equipped with a Charged-Coupled Device (CCD) array, this instrument detects X-rays emitted from high-energy astrophysical sources like supernovae and black holes, with a pointing accuracy of 5'' RA and 0.5'' DEC.

A wide-field camera surveys gamma-ray sources with less stringent pointing requirements, detecting high-energy radiation emitted by astrophysical objects.
