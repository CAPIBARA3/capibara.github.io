About the Project

This project aims to develop and launch a satellite to detect cosmic rays in Earth's orbit. We seek to measure both primary cosmic rays (charged particles) and secondary emissions (X-rays and ùõæ-rays).

Exploring these high-energy radiation, we hope to advance our understanding of astrophysical phenomena and Earth's physics.

We also aim to contribute to fields such as particle physics, climate studies, medicine, planetary science, and more by making our data accessible to the public.

As a student-led project, we are very grateful to the SPARK program for the opportunity and our teachers for their support and dedication.
